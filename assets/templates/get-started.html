Break Timings

  1. For 25 minutes of work or less, take a five-minute break.

  2. For 25-50 minutes of work, take an eight-minute break.

  3. For 50-90 minutes of work, take a 10-minute break.

  4. For more than 90 minutes of work, take a 15-minute break.

Creation of Flow

  • Under Task Section, Type your task name, and click Add Task Button

  • Select the Created Task and Start the flow clock. That's it :)

  • Task will be added like shown above

  • Start the flow clock. That's it

  • Click the Stop Button to stop tracking the time

  • Based on your tracked time, we will set the break time for you.

  • You could also customize the break time

Customization of BreakTime

  • Click on the Timer Text
  • You will get the popup to customize the Break like below
  • Set your custom break time and click change
  • That's it. Break time has been changed
